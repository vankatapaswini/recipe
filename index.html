Delicious Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (with bone preferred)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon)
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, biryani masala)
  • Curd/Yogurt – 1/2 cup
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until partially done.
  5. Layer rice over the chicken, sprinkle coriander and mint, and cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
